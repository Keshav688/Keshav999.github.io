Learn about Naplan

NAPLAN (National Assessment Program – Literacy and Numeracy) is an annual test for Australian students in Years 3, 5, 7, and 9. It assesses core skills in reading, writing, language conventions (spelling, grammar, punctuation), and numeracy. The goal is to track students' progress and ensure they’re meeting key educational standards across the country.

Watch: Where to find Naplan Mock Tests

While NAPLAN provides valuable insights for teachers, parents, and policymakers, it’s just one part of a student’s overall learning journey. Results help identify strengths and areas for improvement, but they don’t define a student’s abilities or school quality. The best preparation is simply learning the curriculum and doing your best on the day.
